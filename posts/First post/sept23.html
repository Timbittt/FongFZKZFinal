First post - Interests

Sept 23, 2025

  1. Reading

    2. Reading is one of my favorite things to do. Just sitting back and relaxing with a good book all day, with no worries or responsibilities. You can experience incredible worlds, step in the shoes of determined characters, and watch them face and overcome impossible challenges. You can discover the wonders of the universe, why it is the way it is and what makes it tick. You can learn about the people who have shaped our world and their experiences. The author can share their greatest joys and sorrows, the very things that make them human, through those sheets of paper. All in all, I look back with nostalgia to reading Jules Verne, Brian Sanderson, The Lord of the Rings, Six of Crows, Tom Sawyer and more, and am eternally grateful for discovering those worlds.

  2. Hiking

    3. My family often goes on hiking trips. We drive out to national or provincial parks, like Bronte Creek, or Wasaga Beach. The drive is often long, but the fun on being out in nature, with no worries or stresses is well worth it.

  3. Computers

    4. Many people look down on screen time, but playing video games, learning, watching interesting videos, and connecting with friends are some of the awesome things you can do with them. Learning to code, building robotics or ardiuno projects and more are some cool things I would like to do more often.

  4. Gardening

    5. I wouldn't really call this a hobby of mine, but I would like to try to get into it. I think everyone has some experience with growing plants - I had an aerogarden that would let you grow plants without soil in water with nutrients. I recently moved houses and would love the opportunity to add more life and greenery to my room, grow my own produce, and help nourish and grow life from the tiny specks that are seeds.
