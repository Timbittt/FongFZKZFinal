Second Post - Driving Questions

Oct 13, 2025

Every project requires direction, purpose and a specific issue that it addresses to be successful. A driving question provides these requirements, giving a clear avenue for experimentation.
After brainstorming potential interests, and creating driving questions for 2 of them, I use AI as a tool to glean feedback and refine them. I used the Google Gemini 2.5 Pro model, providing it the project description, criteria for improvement, and each of the driving questions I brainstormed.

Original Question Ai Feedback Modified Question
What properties do cooling systems take advantage of to create a decrease in temperature? Initial question is not specific and has limited societal connection. How can thermoelectric heat pumps be used to increase the efficiency of cooling systems of server buildings?
How do different materials affect the movement of electromagnetic waves? Very broad. Should be focused on specific problem and product. How do different materials affect the absorption of electromagnetic waves, such as Wi-Fi frequencies, and how can their impacts be measured to optimize connectivity?
Could electrically powered planes be a more environmentally friendly and efficient alternative? Change it from a yes/no research question. Decrease scope of project. Scrapped
How can energy generated through movement be used to power electronics in an emergency scenario? Make more focused. Specify a specific aspect of physics. Could kinetic energy be used to power the electronics used in our day-to-day lives and replace disposable batteries?
How do leverage, friction, centripetal force, air resistance and other relevant forces affect the behavior and functionality of bicycles while cycling? Focus on a single aspect of physics as it relates to cycling How do different bike tire textures and surfaces affect bike performance?

Conclusion

Google Gemini provided some helpful feedback, but there was a lot of repetition and jargon. Additionally, it gave a few suggestions that are unrealistic, such as molding plastic and rubber to engineer new brake pad / tire material. However, it did provide many useful insights and a different perspective that was beneficial for the questions, illustrating the beneficial aspects of AI with human supervision.

Final Choice

How do different materials affect the absorption of electromagnetic waves, such as Wi-Fi frequencies, and how can their impacts be measured to optimize connectivity?
